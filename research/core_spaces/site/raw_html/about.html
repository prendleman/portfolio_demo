About

We’re reinventing purpose-built student housing and build-to-rent homes.

Core Spaces is a vertically integrated ecosystem focused on building, buying, and managing student housing and build-to-rent communities. From new developments to acquisitions, everything we do stems from a culture of innovation and the drive to constantly improve the resident experience.

Vision

To create a better life for everyone under our roof.

Mission

To build a hospitality-minded ecosystem that drives growth for all stakeholders.

Strategy

To be the most iconic owner of real estate whose competitive advantage is a culture of innovation and the resident experience.
