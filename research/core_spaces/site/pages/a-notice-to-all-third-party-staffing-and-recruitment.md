Core Spaces Skip to content Notice &amp; Terms Notice &amp; Terms A Notice to all Third-Party Staffing and Recruitment Agents: Core Spaces, LLC, together with our hiring subsidiaries and affiliates (collectively “Core Spaces”), has a fully dedicated internal Talent Acquisition team to service our hiring needs. Core Spaces may utilize third-party staffing firms, agencies and/or external recruiters (“External Assistance”) on an as-needed basis. Our Talent Acquisition team is to be the point of contact for any External Assistance. External Assistance should NOT directly contact or connect with any Core Spaces employee (outside of our Talent Acquisition team) to present candidates. Core Spaces will NOT accept unsolicited resumes from External Assistance. All unsolicited resumes sent to any Core Spaces email, mailing address, directly to an employee or hiring manager, or to our Applicant Tracking System, will be treated as received directly from the candidate, and we reserve the right to pursue and hire those candidates without financial obligation to External Assistance.
